Vectocade

A Fantasy Computer for making tiny retro games

Vectocade is a small browser bassed fantasy computer for making and playing tiny retro games. Games (also known as carts) are written in JavaScript.

Vectocade is designed to encourage play, creativity and problem solving.

By working in a restricted environment of colours, processing power, graphics, sound and controls; it sparks creativity and problem solving.
